Support Us

Don't forget, you can raise free funds for the Old Schoolhouse everytime you shop online via the fundraising platform Give as you Live.

How it works:

  • You regisiter with Give as you Live.
  • You name the Old Schoolhouse as your preferred charity.
  • You shop online with any of over 4,100 top retailers.
  • Retailers linked with the scheme pay a percentage of any purchase you make back to Give as you Live.
  • Give as you Live then direct at least 50% of that amount to the charity organisation of your choice.

It might sound a bit complicated on first reading but it is straight forward in practice. If all of our supporters were registered with the scheme it would mean a significant source of income for us.

So, please, click on the image below to visit the Give as you Live Website where you can register, name us as one of your chosen charities and find out more information on the scheme - it only takes a few minutes to get set up. Thank you!
