What's on at the Old Schoolhouse?

There's always plenty going on here at the Old Schoolhouse:

Both the Quiet Room and our Snack Shack are open Monday to Friday from 9am to 5pm. Also, don't forget, our gardening group is open to anyone and membership means you can pop in and potter at a time that suits you.

In addition, below you will find a list of regular weekly groups and classes followed by details of those groups that meet less often. Please don't hesitate to give us a call if there's anything you're interested in or would like to know more about.

Other Groups

Singing for the Brain

The Alzheimer's Society's Singing for the Brain takes place from 11.00-13.00 on the 2nd & 4th Thursdays of each month.

For people living with dementia and their carers.

There is no cost and refreshments are provided.

Contact details:
telephone: 0151 426 4433
email: knowsley@alzheimers.org.uk.
Memory Lane

Memory Lane aim to provide emotional and practical support to carers as well as stimulating activities for those with dementia. They run a group here at the Old Schoolhouse on alternate Thursdays from 10-2pm.
