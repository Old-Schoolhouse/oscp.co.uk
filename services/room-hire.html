Room Hire

The Old Schoolhouse offers a unique, friendly and welcoming atmosphere.

Whatever your needs, our high quality rooms provide space and versatility for business meetings, training days, group gatherings, one-to-one sessions or social functions. Most of our meeting rooms have a separate breakout area - offering the facility for both a formal and more relaxed setting for your meetings.

The building is fully accessible with platform lifts to the upper floor and the main hall.

We encourage visitors to enjoy our extensive gardens which lie to the rear of the building. Here you will find relaxation areas, raised beds and vegetable plots.

Projector and flipchart equipment is available for hire and our experienced staff can help with any other requirements you may have to ensure that your meeting is a success.

Refreshments are available at £1.50 per person. (All day meetings £2.00 per person)

Whatever your budget or group size, if you require catering, our in-house chef can meet your needs.
